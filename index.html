About Us

Because We Care About
Your Petroleum Cargo

Hayat Petroleum Trading L.L.C was established in 2023 in the United Arab Emirates with our office located in Dubai. The core of our business is the safe and efficient transportation of petroleum products to the marine industry and commercial outlets. We are rapidly increasing our client base due to our efficient and professional service — driven by our motto: "Because We Care About Your Petroleum Cargo".

Our Dispatch Network has been strategically tailored to cover the UAE's main cities and all major ports. Inspired by our customers' needs, our on-time delivery success rate has reached 90% — unmatched in the region. Competitive pricing, professionalism in transport handling, a well-maintained modern fleet, and the safety of every petroleum shipment is the story of excellence told by Hayat Petroleum Trading L.L.C.
